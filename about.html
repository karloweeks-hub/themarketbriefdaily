About

Independent market research, focused on mechanics.

The Market Brief Daily publishes clear, risk-aware analysis across metals and macro—with emphasis on market structure: inventories, delivery windows, funding/lease rates, positioning, and transmission mechanisms.

What you’ll find here

  • Metals market plumbing: COMEX inventory, delivery dynamics, physical tightness signals.
  • Macro transmission: how rates, FX, and liquidity propagate into commodities and risk assets.
  • Risk framing: scenarios, indicators to monitor, and invalidation conditions.
Editorial stance

No prediction theater. No hype. The focus is on what changed, why it matters, and what to watch next.
